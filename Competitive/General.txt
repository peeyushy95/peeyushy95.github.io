$n\&(n-1)$<pre>                         turn off last set bit.</pre>
$n\&(n+1)$<pre>                         turn off last consecutive run of one.</pre>
$n|(n-1)$<pre>                          turn on last consecutive run of zero.</pre>
$n|(n+1)$<pre>                          turn on last zero bit.</pre>
